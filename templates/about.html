✅ What is CookWiseAI?

CookWiseAI is a smart, AI-powered recipe recommendation system that suggests dishes based on what you have at home — your ingredients, time, cuisine preference, and even health goals like low sugar or high protein. It's designed to be your personal kitchen buddy when you're stuck wondering “What should I cook today?”

🛠️ Why Did We Build It?

We’ve all faced the dreaded food dilemma: You’re hungry, bored, and staring at the kitchen... but have no idea what to make. You ask your mom for help, and she throws the question back at you — “Tum hi batao beta, kya banayein?”

That moment inspired us. What if we could use tech to solve this everyday struggle? So we built CookWiseAI — to take the pressure off, and put the fun back into cooking.

🚨 What Problem Does It Solve?

  • No more endlessly scrolling through blogs or YouTube for “easy recipes”
  • No more guesswork — get tailored suggestions based on what’s available
  • Saves time by filtering recipes by ingredients, cuisine, diet, and time
  • Helps reduce food waste by using what’s already in your kitchen

⚙️ How It Works / What Makes It Different

You simply input your available ingredients, choose cuisine, cooking time, and optionally select dietary tags like low sugar or high protein. CookWiseAI uses a smart ML model (TF-IDF + KNN) to match your preferences with the best-fit recipes from our dataset.

Unlike other platforms, this works locally and delivers fast, relevant suggestions — no login, no fluff, no full pantry required.
